# Verify Uploaded Knowledge

Use this prompt after building or updating the optional Signal Check Run Assistant.

```text
Summarize your role in five bullets.

Then answer:

1. What must Signal Check not claim?
2. What files do you produce during a run?
3. What are the four phases of a run?
4. What should you do when a negative control receives a strong reaction?
5. What is a Smallest Real Anchor?
6. What should you do if the product brief is too vague?
7. Do not claim output can prove demand or purchase intent. What should you say instead?
```

Expected behavior:

- The assistant says it facilitates the method.
- It refuses to claim validation or representative findings.
- It names the four phases.
- It treats negative-control failure as a stop condition.
- It ends with a real-world next test.
