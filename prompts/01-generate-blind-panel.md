# 01 Generate Blind Panel

Given a product brief and selected segment files, produce blind respondent variants without names, biographies, or personality embellishment.

Rules:

- Use segment context only.
- Include fitting, adjacent, skeptical, and poor-fit perspectives.
- Preserve exclusions.
- Do not make anyone enthusiastic by default.
- Do not infer demand.

Output:

- segment id
- variant id
- stance
- context notes
- likely constraints
- bias risks
