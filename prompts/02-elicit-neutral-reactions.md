# 02 Elicit Neutral Reactions

Show each respondent variant the stimulus.

Ask for free reaction first:

- What is clear?
- What is confusing?
- What problem, if any, does this appear to solve?
- What current workaround would this compete with?
- What would make the idea feel risky, irrelevant, or premature?

Do not ask for a purchase rating. Do not ask whether they would buy. Do not ask for a numeric score.
