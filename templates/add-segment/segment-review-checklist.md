# Segment Review Checklist

- The segment is defined by context, not personality.
- There are no names, biographies, avatars, or invented people.
- The segment has clear exclusions.
- The fit type is explicit.
- Current alternatives are named.
- Likely objections are named.
- Trust needs are named.
- Reachability is realistic enough for a Smallest Real Anchor.
- Constraints are relevant to adoption or interpretation.
- The segment is not only a flattering target buyer.
- The segment is not an overfit copy of the founder's desired customer.
- The segment can reject the idea without being treated as wrong.
- The segment can produce useful disagreement.
- At least one skeptical, poor-fit, or non-market-control segment exists in the plan.
- The segment does not imply representative findings or measured demand.
