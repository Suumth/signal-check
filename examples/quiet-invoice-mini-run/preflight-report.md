# Preflight report: Quiet Invoice

## Decision

Test.

Do not build a full product yet.

## Rationale

The idea is clear enough for a small real-world validation step, but not strong enough to justify implementation beyond a narrow test artifact.

## Strongest signal

Freelancers may recognize the emotional discomfort of following up on unpaid invoices.

## Weakest signal

The product may be too small if users only need templates, or too light if users need accounting workflows.

## Kill criteria

Stop or reframe if:

- freelancers do not report recent invoice follow-up discomfort
- people describe the idea as another accounting tool
- the calm tone is appreciated but no one wants a concrete artifact
- negative controls perform as well as the real stimuli

## Claim boundaries

This report must not be used as market research, proof of demand, representative evidence, real customer feedback, or validated purchase intent.
