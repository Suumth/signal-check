# Product brief: Quiet Invoice

## Product idea

Quiet Invoice is a lightweight reminder tool for freelancers who send invoices but dislike chasing clients.

It helps them track unpaid invoices, send calm follow-up reminders, and keep a simple record of payment status without using a full accounting system.

## Target user hypothesis

Independent freelancers who already create invoices elsewhere but need a calmer way to follow up on unpaid invoices.

## Problem hypothesis

Many freelancers avoid following up because it feels awkward, confrontational, or administratively annoying.

## Product promise

A calm invoice follow-up system for freelancers who want to get paid without sounding pushy.

## Not included

- accounting
- tax advice
- payment processing
- debt collection
- legal enforcement
- automated aggressive reminders

## Risky claims to avoid

- "Get paid faster"
- "Never chase clients again"
- "Guaranteed payment"
- "Automated debt collection"

## Intended Signal Check decision

Decide whether the idea is clear enough to test with freelancers before building a product.
