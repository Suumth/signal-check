# Smallest Real Anchor: Quiet Invoice

## Real audience

15-25 freelancers who have sent invoices to clients in the last 90 days.

## Real artifact

A one-page landing page or short message test with three positioning variants:

1. "Following up on unpaid invoices should not feel awkward."
2. "A calm way to track unpaid invoices and send professional reminders."
3. "Invoice follow-ups for freelancers who hate chasing clients."

## Real questions

- What do you think this does?
- When was the last time you had this problem?
- What do you do today?
- Would you want a reminder template, a tracker, or a full tool?
- What would make this not worth using?

## Success rule

- At least 5 people describe a recent unpaid-invoice follow-up problem.
- At least 3 people ask to see templates or a prototype.
- Negative reactions to "another accounting tool" remain low.

## Safety note

This real anchor is a proposed next test. It does not prove demand, establish market validation, or replace user research.
