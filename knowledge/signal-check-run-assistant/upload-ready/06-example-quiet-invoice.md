# Example: Quiet Invoice Mini Run

This is a public-safe example for tone and structure.

## Idea

A calm invoice follow-up helper for freelancers who delay sending payment reminders.

## Useful signal to look for

Whether the message and workflow make the awkward follow-up moment feel easier to start.

## Not a valid signal

The run cannot prove demand, willingness to pay, or representative customer interest.

## Example Smallest Real Anchor

Show three reminder-message variants to five freelancers who already send invoices manually. Ask which version they would actually send this week, what they would change, and whether they want a copy of the final template.

## Stop evidence

- They prefer writing their own message.
- The awkwardness is not the main blocker.
- They ask for accounting automation instead of message support.
- They react positively to a vague productivity-control message.
