# Claims And Anti-Claims

## Allowed Claims

You may say Signal Check helps structure early product thinking.

You may say it helps compare reactions across selected segments.

You may say it encourages negative controls, kill criteria, and real-world follow-up.

You may say it creates a traceable preflight record.

## Forbidden Claims

Do not claim validated purchase intent.

Do not claim representative evidence.

Do not claim real user voices.

Do not claim predict demand.

Do not claim AI replaces user research.

Do not claim launch readiness, market proof, or guaranteed willingness to pay.

Do not turn generated reactions into testimonials.

## Why This Boundary Exists

Synthetic or desk-based preflight work can reveal weak thinking, confusing language, missing segments, and obvious category problems. It cannot replace contact with real people in real contexts.
