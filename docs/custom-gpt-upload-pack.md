# Custom GPT Upload Pack

This document lists the files to upload to the Custom GPT Knowledge section.

```text
knowledge/signal-check-run-assistant/rules.md
knowledge/signal-check-run-assistant/phase-map.md

```

## Do not upload

Do not upload:

- private customer data
- internal product strategy
- raw user interviews
- private repositories
- unreleased product specs
- vault files
- screenshots or media assets
- large raw responses

## Upload rationale

The knowledge pack is intentionally compact. It keeps retrieval focused and avoids making the Custom GPT depend on the entire repository.
